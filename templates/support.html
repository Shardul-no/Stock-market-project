Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is fundamental analysis?

Fundamental analysis is a method of evaluating a company's intrinsic value by examining its financial statements, management team, industry trends, and competitive position.

2. How do I conduct fundamental analysis?

To conduct fundamental analysis, gather financial data, analyze financial statements, evaluate management, assess industry trends, and determine the company’s competitive position.

3. What are the benefits of fundamental analysis?

Fundamental analysis helps investors make informed decisions, create long-term wealth, and manage risk effectively.
